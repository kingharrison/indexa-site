Process

Three steps from knowledge to delivery.

01 ——

Build Your RAG

Import documents, PDFs, text, and data sources. Indexa indexes and organizes your knowledge into a queryable RAG bundle.

02 ——

Encrypt & Export

Lock the bundle with strong encryption. Export a sealed file — your original sources stay completely private, never embedded in plain form.

03 ——

Share & Query

Recipients import the encrypted bundle into Indexa and query it freely. They get the answers — never the sources. Your intellectual property stays yours.

Features

Built for sharing knowledge without surrendering it.

End-to-End Encryption

RAG bundles are encrypted at rest. Recipients query the knowledge without any ability to extract or view the underlying source documents.

Portable RAG Bundles

Export your knowledge base as a single encrypted file. Share it via email, cloud storage, or USB — no server required.

One-Click Import

Recipients import a bundle and start querying immediately. No setup, no configuration, no account required.

Multi-Source Indexing

Ingest documents, PDFs, web pages, and text files. Indexa normalizes and indexes them into a unified, queryable knowledge base.

Version Management

Track and manage multiple versions of your RAG bundles. Recipients always know which version they're querying.

Desktop Native

A clean, fast native desktop app for Mac, Windows, and Linux. No browser, no cloud dependency — everything runs locally on your machine.

Who It's For

Your knowledge, in the right hands — and nowhere else.

Consultants

Share proprietary research and methodology with clients. They get answers, you keep your IP.

Enterprises

Distribute internal knowledge bases to partners or vendors without exposing source documents or confidential data.

Educators

Developers
